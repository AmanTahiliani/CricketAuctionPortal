What is Cricplay

CricPlay is a User-Friendly auction Portal where teams can have bidding wars over the world's best players to build your Dream team.

Instructions to use

The auctioneer will assign each team their own bid raising button and the highest bid after a 10 sec window of the last bid raised wins the player!
